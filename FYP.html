Computers learning Speech

For my final project during my stay at staffordshire university I wanted to exlore artificial inteligence. Through the use of neural networks the project aimed to recreate human speech live, in the form of an audiovisual installation.


This project was not possible to complete without the help and guidance of my teacher, Simon Waite. My friends and collagues helped me reflect and refine the project so I will thank Marco Tarantino, Christian Popple, Joe Tonkinson and many more. And of course I will thank my beloved for allowing me to go on with this project, I stressed her at every opportunity and pushed her to her limit
